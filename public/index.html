Rules

Super Tic-Tac-Toe is an advanced version of Tic-Tac-Toe played on a 9x9 grid.

  • The 9x9 grid is divided into nine 3x3 grids, known as mini-boards.
  • The first player (X) starts by playing in any of the 81 spots.
  • The location of the move within a mini-board determines where the next player (O) must play. For example, if X plays in the top-left corner of the top-left mini-board, O must play in the top-left mini-board.
  • If a mini-board is already won or full, the player can choose any other mini-board.
  • Players win a mini-board by getting three of their marks in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally).
  • The game is won by the player who wins three mini-boards in a row on the larger 3x3 grid.
  • The game ends in a draw if all mini-boards are full and no player has won the larger grid.
