Rules

  • The game is played on a 3x3 grid of tic-tac-toe boards.
  • Players take turns making moves within the mini boards.
  • The first move can be made on any board and in any space.
  • Subsequent moves must be made on the mini board corresponding to the previous player's move.
  • Winning a mini board awards the player that board.
  • The first player to win three mini boards in a row wins the game.
